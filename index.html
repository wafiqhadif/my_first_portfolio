Projects

CORTEIZ

Corteiz is a London-based streetwear brand founded in 2017 by Clint419. Known for its bold designs and underground style, it built hype through exclusivity and unique marketing. The label quickly became a cultural movement, attracting fans worldwide with limited drops, street credibility, and a rebellious spirit.

Review Brand

SUPREME

Supreme is a New York City streetwear brand founded in 1994 by James Jebbia. Originally a skate shop in Manhattan, it quickly became known for its bold box logo, limited releases, and strong ties to skate culture. Over the years, Supreme expanded globally, building a cult following and collaborating with major fashion houses.

Review Brand

PALACE

Palace Skateboards is a London-based brand founded in 2009 by Lev Tanju. Starting from the UK skate scene, it gained recognition for its unique graphics, VHS-style videos, and bold logo. Over time, Palace grew into a global streetwear label, mixing skate culture with high fashion collaborations.

Review Brand

HUMAN MADE

Human Made is a Japanese fashion label founded in 2010 by Nigo, the creator of BAPE. The brand blends vintage Americana, workwear, and playful graphics, often featuring animal motifs. Known for its high-quality craftsmanship and retro feel, Human Made has become a global streetwear staple with strong cultural influence.

Review Brand

COLE BUXTON

Cole Buxton is a London-based brand founded in 2014 by Cole Buxton and Jonny Wilson. Originally focused on refined menswear, it later redefined itself through “Athletic Essentials,” merging sportswear with luxury minimalism. The label is known for clean silhouettes, premium materials, and a strong presence in modern street culture.

Review Brand

AIMÉ LEON DORE

Aimé Leon Dore is a New York-based fashion brand founded in 2014 by Teddy Santis. Known for blending streetwear with classic menswear, it draws inspiration from Queens culture, basketball, and ’90s aesthetics. The label gained global recognition through its clean designs, storytelling, and collaborations with major brands like New Balance.
